⚙️ How it Works

From Upload to Insight in Seconds

Our RAG pipeline processes your documents and makes them searchable by an LLM in real time.

1

Create Your Account

Register securely with email and password. Data encrypted in PostgreSQL with bcrypt hashing.

2

Upload Research Papers

Drag and drop PDFs. The system chunks text and builds a vector index automatically.

3

Ask Your Questions

Type any research question in plain English. RAG retrieves relevant chunks and feeds them to the LLM.

4

Get Grounded Answers

Receive accurate, cited answers drawn directly from your uploaded papers.
